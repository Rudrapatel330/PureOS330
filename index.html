ABOUT PURE OS

Pure OS started as a hobby project to design a high-fidelity, responsive graphical operating system written completely from scratch. Combining low-level assembly drivers with a modern double-buffered UI renderer, Pure OS challenges the limits of custom kernel environments.

Our team believes in open-source systems, performant design parameters, and pixel-perfect layouts scaled directly to hardware profiles.
